There's a certain poetry in the act of giving away power. Meta's release of LlamaAgent 2.0 under the MIT license isn't just a technical decision—it's a philosophical statement about the nature of innovation and progress.

In an era where AI capabilities are increasingly concentrated in the hands of a few, this move represents a counter-current. It's a bet that collective intelligence, enabled by open access, will ultimately surpass closed systems.
